Perhitungan Determinan Matriks

1. Matriks 2x2

\( A = \begin{pmatrix} -7 & -5 \\ 1 & 4 \end{pmatrix} \)

\( \det(A) = (-7)(4) - (-5)(1) = -23 \)

2. Matriks 3x3

\( A = \begin{pmatrix} 0 & 2 & -3 \\ 1 & -2 & -1 \\ 0 & 0 & 1 \end{pmatrix} \)

Ekspansi baris pertama:

\( \det(A) = 2(-1)^{1+2} \begin{vmatrix} 1 & -1 \\ 0 & 1 \end{vmatrix} + (-3)(-1)^{1+3} \begin{vmatrix} 1 & -2 \\ 0 & 0 \end{vmatrix} \)

\( \det(A) = -2 \)

3. Matriks 4x4

\( A = \begin{pmatrix} 1 & -3 & 1 & 1 \\ -3 & 1 & 1 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & -3 & 1 \\ 1 & 1 & 1 & -3 \end{pmatrix} \)

\( \det(A) = -256 \)
